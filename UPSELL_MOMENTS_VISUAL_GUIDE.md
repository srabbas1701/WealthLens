## Design Principles

### Visual Consistency
- ✅ Same card style as dashboard
- ✅ Consistent border radius (12px)
- ✅ Same color palette
- ✅ Professional typography

### Non-Intrusive
- ✅ Inline placement
- ✅ No pop-ups
- ✅ No blocking
- ✅ Respectful spacing

### Trust-First
- ✅ Calm language
- ✅ Clear value
- ✅ No urgency
- ✅ User choice
